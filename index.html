Product Management Intern - Innovations

LogMeIn Innovations is a special team focused on finding the next moon shot product for LogMeIn. Over the course of three months, I worked with a small team of product managers, UX designers, software engineers, and data scientists to validate user problems and design product solutions using agile methodology and lean startup thinking. One of these product that we developed and tested over the summer is Scopist.io. Scopist is a new AI bot which will record your meetings and capture the key decisions, facts, and action items so that you can focus on collaboration during the meeting. During my time, I analyzed user research to create journey maps and personas to understand user habits and hone in on the key pain points that our new product would solve. I also conducted market research to understand the product's position in the field , estimate pricing, and identify opportunity areas for growth. Finally, I wrote new user stories and led grooming sessions to focus development on high priority targets with the help of the product manager and the engineering team.

  • Company: LogMeIn
  • Date: June 2017

Academic Technology Fellow

The Academic Technology Fellows work supports the IT department at Tufts University by prototyping and maintaining new technology-based solutions to enhance teaching and learning at Tufts. With a team of designers and engineers, I built a natural language processing application to digest data and analyze trends to help Tufts University administrators make decisions. Some of the other projects I have worked on include helping Tufts researchers display their data on custom made Google maps and created guides to help Tufts professors better use the technologies offered by the university.

Co-Founder

The Tufts Entrepreneurs Society serves the Tufts community by maintaining the VentureLab, an incubator on campus, and building a more visible and active entrepreneurial community. In the past year, we have grown the incubator from 15 to 38 startups and created a network of over 70 mentors to advise and push our student ventures forward. I organized and ran office hours, coaching nights, pitch nights, and marketing campaigns while also consulting individual startups in the venture lab and connecting them with the resources they need. You can find more information at our website.
